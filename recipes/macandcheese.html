Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A classic, homemade, mac and cheese recipe. Plenty of cheese while keeping it simple. Enjoy bringing the centerpiece of your next family gathering with this amazing dish.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 2 tablespoons
  • Butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Ground Pepper - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Elbow Paste Noodles - 16 ounces
  • Extra Sharp Cheddar Cheese - 6-8 ounces shredded
  • Mozzerella Cheese - 6-8 ounces shredded

Directions

  1. In a moderate sized saucepan, set heat to medium-low and add butter
  2. Preheat milk in microwave for 2 minutes
  3. After butter is melted, slowly add flour and mix
  4. Continue to stir and heat for 1-2 minutes until slightly browned
  5. Slowly add milk about 1/2 a cup at a time. Continue to stir and give time for sauce to thicken
  6. Continue to cook and stir until bubbles form in the sauce. Cook about 2 minutes longer
  7. Add salt and pepper to sauce
  8. Add 6-8 ounces of cheese to taste and consistency desired. Cheddar for more color and flavor. Mozzerella for thickening and consistency
  9. Reduce heat to low
  10. In a separate pot, bring about 6 cups of water to a boil
  11. Add pasta to water and continue to boil for about 8 minutes
  12. Drain water from pasta and add the pasta to sauce
  13. Mix thoroughly
  14. In an 8 by 10 baking dish, add half of the pasta and sauce mixture
  15. Spread the remainder of the mozzerella cheese onto the dish
  16. Add the rest of the pasta and sauce to the dish
  17. Spread the remainer of the cheddar cheese onto the macaroni
  18. Chill until needed or proceed to baking
  19. Bake at 400 F for 20 minutes or until cheese is golden brown

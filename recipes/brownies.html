Brownies

Description

These cake-like brownies are the most delicious, fudgy treats I have ever eaten. They are super quick and easy to make. For best results, use Dutch-processed cocoa. For less cake-like brownies, use all purpose flour instead of self-rising.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup self-rising flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (Optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F. Grease and flour an 8x8- or 9x9-inch baking pan.
  2. Beat together sugar and butter in a medium bowl. Add eggs and mix well. Combine flour, cocoa, and salt; stir into sugar mixture. Mix in vanilla, then stir in walnuts. Spread evenly into prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are firm, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool before cutting into squares.
